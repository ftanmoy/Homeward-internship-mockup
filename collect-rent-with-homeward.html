How to collect rent with Homeward

Collecting rent online with Homeward is simple, safe, and only takes a few minutes to set up.

Step 1: You'll Need To Add Your Property

On the homescreen, tap the "Add Property" button.

You will need to enter your property information, such as property type, number of units, and property name. Even if you do not have a formal name, it will help you distinguish this property from your other ones.

Next, you need to fill in the address and add a picture if you would like. Once finished, you could see the different units on the screen.

Step 2: Add The Lease Information

To add lease info, select one of the vacant units and press on the ” start collecting rent” button.

You will need to enter the Lease information; the lease start date, end date, or the month-to-month option.

Step 3: Connect Your Bank Account

At Homeward, we understand the importance of top-notch security, that is why we go above and beyond to put your privacy first. We follow all PCI compliance and federal regulations for your safety and work with leaders in data and banking security to ensure that your data is completely safe during this entire process.

Watch the video below to learn how to connect your bank account with Homeward.

Step 4: Enter Your Rent Information

After adding the bank account, the next step will be to enter the rent information; this includes the related bank account, rent amount, monthly due day, and late fees. You will also have the option to add a one-time payment. For example, security deposit or utility fees, which you can also add later.

Note that you can have different bank accounts linked to separate leases, so when you add the rent information choose the relevant bank.

Step 4: Invite Your Tenants

Finally, it is time to invite your tenants. All you need is their full name and phone number. E-mail is optional but highly recommended for a successful onboarding process.

Once you send the invite, your tenants will receive a text message with a link to download the app. Only invited tenants can be associated with your properties.

To receive rent on time, Homeward offers your tenants an option to set up automatic rent payments and send reminders before the rent is due, raising the chances of getting your rent on time and avoiding any unnecessary confusion or mishaps.

Once their rent is being processed, you will receive a notification. It will take 2-3 business days to receive the payment, and yes, it is free for both sides, making rent collection a breeze for all.
